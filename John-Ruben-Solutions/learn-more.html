Metabolic Protocols

Nutrition isn't just about calories. It's about engineering your cellular function to lower inflammation and optimize performance.

The Science of Metabolism

Chronic inflammation is the "silent" driver behind metabolic dysfunction. The good news? You can actively lower this flame with the right inputs.

Protocol 01

Omega 6:3 Ratio

Target: 4:1 Ratio

The modern diet is pro-inflammatory (20:1). Achieving a 4:1 ratio is associated with a 70% reduction in total mortality in cardiovascular studies.

Protocol 02

Time-Restricted Feeding

Target: 18:6 Window

Fasting for 18 hours improves insulin sensitivity and reduces fat mass without muscle loss (provided protein is sufficient). It dictates your hormonal environment.

Protocol 03

Creatine Monohydrate

Focus: Muscle & Brain

Crucial for cellular energy (ATP) and cognitive health. The ISSN confirms its efficacy in preserving lean mass during weight loss phases.

Protocol 04

Zone 2 & Resistance

Focus: Mitochondria

Zone 2 improves mitochondrial flexibility (fat burning). Combined with lifting, it prevents the "skinny fat" adaptation often seen with diet alone.

Protocol 05 - The Foundation

Sleep & Hydration

Metabolic dysregulation begins with poor recovery. Sleep curtailment (< 7.5 hrs) decreases insulin sensitivity and increases hunger hormones (ghrelin). Hydration is critical for liver function.
